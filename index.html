The 3-Minute Connection Formula

Use the **S.E.T. Opener** at networking events or in quick office interactions:

  • Signal Attention: Acknowledge them with eye contact.
  • Engagement: Ask an open, non-generic question.
  • Topic: Pivot to a specific professional topic of mutual interest.
‚ùì

High-Leverage Questions

Move beyond "What do you do?" to spark real conversation. Examples include:

  • "What is the biggest challenge your team is focused on solving right now?"
  • "What emerging trend are you most excited about in our industry?"
  • "What's one piece of advice you'd give your younger self about navigating this company?"
